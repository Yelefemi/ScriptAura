About The Writers' Forge

The Writers' Forge is a creative imprint dedicated to shaping world-class screenwriters. We believe in excellence, discipline, and collaboration. Our mission is to refine talent, elevate craft, and connect writers to global opportunities. We provide the tools, mentorship, and visibility to help screenwriters thrive. This is where screenwriters become professionals. This is The Forge.

What We Offer:

  • Intensive 13 weeks workshops in screenwriting
  • Access into the Writers' pool
  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Production support for alumni projects
  • Assistance with pitch decks for producers and investors
