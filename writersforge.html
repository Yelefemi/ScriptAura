About The Writers' Forge

The Writer’s Forge is a creative imprint dedicated to shaping world-class screenwriters. We believe in excellence, discipline, and collaboration. Our mission is to refine talent, elevate craft, and connect writers to global opportunities. We provide the tools, mentorship, and visibility to help screenwriters thrive. This is where screenwriters become professionals. This is The Forge.

What We Offer:

  • Intensive programs in writing, directing, producing, and distribution
  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Production support for alumni projects
  • Access to global distribution channels
