About ScriptAura Studios

A modular creative enterprise shaping the future of African storytelling through training, innovation, and global partnerships.

Our Mission

To empower storytellers with the tools, training, and opportunities they need to thrive in the global creative economy.

Our Vision

To build Africa’s leading creative ecosystem where stories and talents are nurtured and exported to the world.

Closed-Loop Ecosystem

From training to production to distribution, we support creators end-to-end.

Talent Pipeline

We develop market-ready screenwriters, actors, and voice talent with global relevance.

Global Reach

Building partnerships that connect African creativity to international opportunities.

Innovation

Leveraging technology and creative strategies to shape the future of storytelling.

Meet Our Executives

Olayinde Ayinde

Chief Executive Officer

Olayinka is a filmmaker, creative strategist, and founder of ScriptAura Studios. With a passion for building Africa’s creative infrastructure, she brings a visionary approach to storytelling, talent development, and industry innovation. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, Olayinka leads ScriptAura Studios as a modular enterprise designed to scale across the continent and beyond.

Dr. Sam Uche Anyamele

Chief Strategy and creative Partnership Officer (CSCP)

C Sam is an industry veteran with decades of experience in filmmaking, creative development, and strategic partnerships. As Chief Strategy & Creative Partnerships Officer, he brings deep insight, credibility, and leadership to ScriptAura Studios, helping shape its vision and connect it to global opportunities. His presence signals a new era of African creative enterprise — bold, collaborative, and future-facing.
