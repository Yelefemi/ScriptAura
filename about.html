Meet Our Executives

Olayinde Ayinde

Chief Executive Officer

Olayinka is a filmmaker, creative entrepreneur, and founder of ScriptAura Studios. With a passion for building global standard creative infrastructure, she brings a visionary approach to storytelling, talent development, and industry innovation. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, Olayinka leads ScriptAura Studios as a modular enterprise designed to scale across the continent and beyond.

Sam Uche Anyamele (Ph.D)

Chief Strategy and Creative Partnership Officer (CSCP)

Sam is an industry veteran with decades of experience in filmmaking, creative development, and partnerships. A seasoned strategist and creative executive, he brings deep insight, credibility, and leadership to ScriptAura Studios, helping shape its vision and connect it to global opportunities. His presence signals a new era of African creative enterprise - bold, collaborative, and future-facing.

Oluwafemi Owoyele

Chief Technology Officer

Femi is a tech-savvy innovator with experience in platform design and a passion for User experience. He oversees ScriptAura Studios’ digital framework, so that the company’s technology infrastructure supports its creative ambition.

Stella Adetola

Jeffery Egblomasse
