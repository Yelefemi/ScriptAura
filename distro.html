ScriptAura Distro

ScriptAura Distro is designed as the producer-empowerment arm of ScriptAura Studios. Its focus is on empowering Producers and Executive Producers (EPs) with the tools, networks, and knowledge to succeed in the filmmaking business.

About ScriptAura Distro

ScriptAura Distro is a subsidiary of ScriptAura Studios, created to bridge the gap between creativity and commerce in filmmaking. We empower producers and executive producers with the business tools, networks, and mentorship they need to succeed — ensuring films are not only made but also market-ready and profitable.

Services

  • Film Business Education
  • Project consulting & packaging
  • Access to distributors, streamers and funders
  • Legal & marketing guidance
